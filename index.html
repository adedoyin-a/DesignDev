Hello, I'm Adedoyin Adebayo

Digital Product and Brand Designer

My journey to digital design began when I was an undergraduate at the university some years ago. I discovered graphic design and fell in love with it. Later on, I took a college course on web page design and soon realized that I enjoyed it as well. Over the years, I have grown as a Designer. I believe that there is always something to learn and an opportunity to improve. Currently, I specialize in User Interface and User Experience Design (UI/UX) and Brand Identity Design. My tools include Figma, Adobe XD, Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Javascript, etc.
